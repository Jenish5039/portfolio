Problem
Problem & Goal
Understanding key user challenges and defining a clear solution direction.
⚠
Problem

Readers struggle to find trustworthy, in-depth analysis on geopolitics and global events. Most news is fragmented, sensationalised, or buried under ads and noise. There is no single platform built for readers who want to truly understand — not just scroll through headlines.

▶
Goal

Design a distraction-free editorial platform where readers can discover long-form geopolitical and climate content, follow connected article series, and build real understanding — with zero ad clutter, a clear visual language, and a trustworthy editorial voice.

Key Features
Key Features
Key features designed to simplify discovery, build trust, and enable deep reading.
01
Series format
Connected multi-part articles let readers follow a story over time with clear progress tracking and part navigation.
02
Category filtering
Tag-based filters for Geopolitics, Climate, Economics, Conflict, and Technology for focused discovery.
03
Zero-ad reading
Completely distraction-free article pages. No ads, no popups, no sidebar noise — just the content.
04
Visual data design
Stats bars, tables, pull quotes, and cliffhanger boxes turn complex data into readable narrative.
05
Save & revisit
Readers bookmark articles to build their own personal knowledge library over time.
06
Night mode
Full dark mode support for comfortable long-form reading at any hour of the day.
